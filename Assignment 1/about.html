About Mulex

Mulex is a visionary gaming company that was established in 2004 by its founder, Mubarak Hassan. The company was born out of Mubarak's deep-rooted passion for video games and programming.

Founder's Background

Mubarak Hassan has been a video game enthusiast since his early childhood. He spent countless hours playing and exploring various video games, immersing himself in the virtual worlds created by game developers. This passion drove him to learn programming and game development, starting with simple game mods and projects.

Over the years, Mubarak honed his skills in programming and game design, eventually leading him to establish Mulex in 2004. His unique combination of gaming experience and technical expertise forms the foundation of the company's vision and success.

Company Vision

Mulex's vision is to be recognized as one of the best gaming companies in the industry. The company is dedicated to creating immersive, entertaining, and innovative video games that captivate players from around the world. With a strong focus on quality and player experience, Mulex is committed to pushing the boundaries of gaming and delivering exceptional entertainment.

Our team of talented developers and designers works tirelessly to bring Mubarak's vision to life, creating games that bring joy and excitement to the gaming community. We look forward to continuing this journey and making a lasting impact on the gaming industry.
